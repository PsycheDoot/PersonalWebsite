Flashing Glasses - Mk4

I haven't made these yet, although I have an idea for the glasses. The lenses will be partially obscured with an 8x8 array of pixels and can be controlled using 2 8-bit shift registers and 2 8-channel multiplexors. In theory, a controller would send serial data to the shift register and the multiplexor would then determine the appropriate row to output the data. The multiplexor would probably be connected to an 3-bit counter circuit that the controller would increment, essentially telling the circuit which row to display.
